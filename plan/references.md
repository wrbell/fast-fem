## Free Online Courses & Lectures

### University Courses (Free)
- **[UMich — The Finite Element Method for Problems in Physics (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/finite-element-method)** — ~45 hrs of lectures. Free to audit. Your own university's course.
- **[UMich Open.Michigan — Intro to FEM](https://open.umich.edu/find/open-educational-resources/engineering/introduction-finite-element-methods)** — Free educational resources
- **[MIT OCW 2.092 — FEA of Solids and Fluids I](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/2-092-finite-element-analysis-of-solids-and-fluids-i-fall-2009/)** — Intro FEM for structural/fluid problems
- **[MIT OCW 2.094 — FEA of Solids and Fluids II](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/2-094-finite-element-analysis-of-solids-and-fluids-ii-spring-2011/)** — Advanced: nonlinear analyses
- **[MIT OCW RES.2-002 — K.J. Bathe's FEM Procedures (video lectures)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/res-2-002-finite-element-procedures-for-solids-and-structures-spring-2010/)** — Comprehensive video series from a legend in the field
- **[Open University — Intro to FEA](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/science-maths-technology/introduction-finite-element-analysis/content-section-0)** — Free, no sign-up, can earn Statement of Participation
- **[Imperial College — Finite Element Course](https://finite-element.github.io/)** — Focuses on writing FE software in Python. 2026 docs available.

### Free Textbooks & Lecture Notes
- **[Carlos Felippa — Intro to FEM (IFEM) Notes](https://quickfem.com/wp-content/uploads/IFEM.Ch00.pdf)** — Legendary notes from CU Boulder, taught since 1986. The gold standard for free FEM theory.
- **[G.P. Nikishkov — Introduction to FEM](https://www.iitg.ac.in/mech/documents/128/introfem.pdf)** — Concise, Galerkin-focused formulation
- **[Larson & Bengzon — The Finite Element Method](https://www2.math.uconn.edu/~leykekhman/courses/MATH_5520/fa_2017/Mats%20Larson,%20Bengzon,%20Theory,%20Imlementation%20and%20Applications.pdf)** — Comprehensive 1D/2D text
- **[P. Seshu — Textbook of Finite Element Analysis](https://soaneemrana.com/onewebmedia/TEXT%20BOOKOF%20FINITE%20ELEMENT%20ANALYSIS%20BY%20P.%20SESHU%20(1).pdf)** — Full FEA textbook
- **[Interactive Intro to FEM (Jupyter notebooks)](https://jschoeberl.github.io/iFEM/intro.html)** — Hands-on with Netgen/NGSolve

## Quick-Start Reading Order (for break week theory sprint)

1. Carlos Felippa IFEM notes Ch. 1–3 (free, best intro to FEM theory)
2. Hughes Ch. 1–3 (weak form, shape functions)
3. Cengel Ch. 2 (thermal)
4. Shigley Ch. 6 + Ch. 9.7–9.9 (fatigue + weld fatigue)
5. Hughes Ch. 7–8 (beams, plates, shells)
6. Inman Ch. 4–6, 9 (vibrations overview)
7. Metal Fatigue Handbook Ch. 5–8 (weld specifics)
